Sausage and Zucchini

Description

This zucchini and sausage recipe goes in the quick and easy category... And yummy, too! Serve over cooked rice or pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds italian sausage links
  • 2 small zucchini, sliced
  • 1 small yellow squash, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 can stewed tomatoes, with liquid

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook sausage until no longer pink inside. Cut sausage into 1/4-inch slices, and continue cooking until browned.
  2. Stir in zucchini, yellow squash, and onion; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Pour in tomatoes with liquid. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
